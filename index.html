I am a communication and information designer, currently Data Designer at Fjord Dublin / The Dock, formerly postdoc research fellow at DensityDesign lab, Politecnico di Milano.

I have been teaching and working mostly with data visualisation and interface design, but I am keen about any application of visual design to interdisciplinary research and complex phenomena.
