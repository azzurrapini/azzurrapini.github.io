Azzurra Pini

I am a design lead with a focus on data driven products and experiences.


I work at the intersection of technology, communication and data design, supporting people and companies navigating complex problems and ecosystems.

My areas of expertise include service & systems design, UX/UI, information design and data visualisation.


Recently I was Data Design Lead at Linktree, at Fjord Dublin / The Dock, and before postdoc research fellow at DensityDesign lab, Politecnico di Milano.


I hold a PhD in Design focusing on the visualisation of organisational processes.

I have been teaching and working particularly with data visualisation and interface design and I am particularly interested into the design of decision support interfaces and human + AI collaboration.
